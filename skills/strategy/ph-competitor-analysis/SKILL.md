# Product Hunt Competitor Analysis

This skill helps you analyze the competitive landscape on Product Hunt, learn from successful launches, and position your product for maximum impact.

## When to Use This Skill

- Researching similar products that launched on Product Hunt
- Evaluating competition for a specific launch day
- Learning from top performers in your category
- Benchmarking your assets against successful launches
- Understanding what makes products win POTD

## Analysis Framework

### 1. Category Research

Identify products in your space that launched successfully:

**Research Sources:**
- Product Hunt search by category/topic
- Product Hunt "Topics" pages
- hunted.space for historical data
- ProductFlair for inspiration

**Data to Collect:**
| Product | Launch Date | Upvotes | Comments | Rank | Tagline |
|---------|-------------|---------|----------|------|---------|
| [Name] | [Date] | [#] | [#] | [#] | [Text] |

### 2. Launch Day Competition Analysis

For your target launch date, research:

**Historical Pattern (Same Day of Week):**
- Average upvotes for #1 position
- Typical number of featured products
- Quality level of competitors

**Benchmark Thresholds (2024-2025 Data):**
| Day | #1 Position | Top 5 | Featured |
|-----|-------------|-------|----------|
| Monday | 500-700 | 300+ | 150+ |
| Tuesday | 600-900 | 400+ | 200+ |
| Wednesday | 700-1000 | 450+ | 200+ |
| Thursday | 600-900 | 400+ | 200+ |
| Friday | 400-600 | 250+ | 150+ |
| Saturday | 300-500 | 200+ | 100+ |
| Sunday | 300-500 | 200+ | 100+ |

### 3. Top Performer Deep Dive

For each successful competitor, analyze:

**Content Elements:**
- Tagline: What makes it compelling?
- Thumbnail: Static or GIF? Style?
- Gallery: How many images? Video included?
- Description: Length, structure, tone
- Maker Comment: Structure, authenticity, CTA

**Engagement Patterns:**
- First hour upvotes
- Comment velocity
- Maker response rate
- Community discussion quality

**Unique Differentiators:**
- What made this product stand out?
- Any special offers or promotions?
- Notable endorsements or hunters?

### 4. Competitive Positioning Matrix

Map your product against competitors:

```
                    HIGH NOVELTY

    AUDIENCE -------|----------------|---- AUDIENCE
```

**Positioning Questions:**
- What's your unique angle vs. existing solutions?
- Why should PH users care about another [category] tool?
- What's your "10x better" story?

## Competitive Intelligence Checklist

### Before Launch
- [ ] Analyzed 5+ similar products in category
- [ ] Documented top 3 POTD winners in niche
- [ ] Identified common tagline patterns
- [ ] Collected best-performing thumbnail styles
- [ ] Noted average engagement benchmarks
- [ ] Identified gaps/opportunities in positioning

### Launch Day Awareness
- [ ] Checked who else is launching that day
