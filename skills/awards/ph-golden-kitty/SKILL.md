# Product Hunt Golden Kitty Awards

This skill helps you understand and strategize for the annual Product Hunt Golden Kitty Awards, the platform's highest recognition.

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning your launch for award eligibility
- Preparing for nomination period
- Mobilizing community for voting
- Understanding award categories

## What Are the Golden Kitty Awards?

### Overview

- Annual celebration of best products
- Recognizes products launched that year
- Community voting determines winners
- Winners receive Golden Kitty trophy

### Timeline (Typical)

- **Nominations:** Early December
- **Voting:** Mid-December
- **Winners Announced:** Late December/Early January

### What Winners Get

- üèÜ Golden Kitty Trophy (physical)
- Product Hunt swag
- Permanent badge on profile
- Major credibility boost
- PR and media coverage

## Strategy for Winning

### Phase 1: Launch Strategy (Pre-Award)

**Launch Timing:**
- Launching early in year = longer community presence
- Launching late = fresher in memory
- Either can work; focus on quality

**Build Community:**
- Strong launch builds foundation
- Engage with supporters post-launch
- Maintain PH presence throughout year

**Track Record:**
- Multiple successful launches help
- Consistent community engagement
- Reputation building

### Phase 2: Nomination Period

**When Nominations Open:**
- Products can be nominated by community
- Top nominations advance to voting
- Category matters for positioning

**Getting Nominated:**
- Previous supporters often nominate
- Community remembers strong launches
- Makers can't self-nominate (usually)

**Increasing Nomination Chances:**
- Strong launch performance
- Active community engagement
- Memorable product/story

### Phase 3: Voting Period

**How Voting Works:**
- Community votes in each category
- Each user can vote once per category
- Votes from quality accounts count more
- Voting typically lasts 1-2 weeks
