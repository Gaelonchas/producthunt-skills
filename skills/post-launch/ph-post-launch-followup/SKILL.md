# Product Hunt Post-Launch Follow-Up

This skill helps you execute effective follow-up activities after your Product Hunt launch to maximize long-term value.

## When to Use This Skill

- The day after your launch
- Thanking supporters and community
- Collecting reviews on other platforms
- Following up with new signups
- Maintaining launch momentum

## Post-Launch Timeline

### Day 1 (Launch Day Ends)

**Immediate Actions:**
- [ ] Post final thank you on PH comments
- [ ] Share results on social media
- [ ] Send thank you email to supporters
- [ ] Document final metrics
- [ ] Screenshot rankings and badges

### Day 2-3

**Follow-Up Actions:**
- [ ] Personal thanks to top commenters
- [ ] Respond to any remaining comments
- [ ] Email new signups (welcome sequence)
- [ ] Reach out to interested prospects
- [ ] Begin feedback collection

### Week 1

**Consolidation:**
- [ ] Request reviews on G2/Capterra
- [ ] Share launch retrospective
- [ ] Implement quick wins from feedback
- [ ] Engage with ongoing discussions
- [ ] Claim product hub

### Week 2-4

**Momentum Maintenance:**
- [ ] Publish launch case study
- [ ] Follow up with unconverted signups
- [ ] Build relationships with active users
- [ ] Plan for potential relaunch

## Collecting Reviews

### Why Reviews Matter

Post-launch is perfect for reviews because:
- Users are most engaged
- Experience is fresh
- Launch momentum is high
- Supporters are willing to help

## Feedback Collection

### What to Ask

**Product Feedback:**
- What made you sign up?
- What's your first impression?
- What's confusing or unclear?
- What feature would you add?

**Launch Feedback:**
- How did you find us on PH?
- What convinced you to try us?
- What almost stopped you?

## Claiming Your Product Hub

### What Is It?

Product Hunt creates a "product hub" for launched products. Claiming it gives you:
- Manage your product page
- Followers from upvoters
- Notification to followers for future launches

### How to Claim

1. Go to your product page
2. Click "Claim this product"
3. Verify ownership
4. Update product info as needed

### Benefits

- All upvoters become followers
- Send updates to followers
- Relaunch notifications automatic
- Build ongoing presence

- Comment on new products

- Share your learnings
- Build relationships
