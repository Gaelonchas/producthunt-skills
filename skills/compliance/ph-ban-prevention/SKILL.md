# Product Hunt Ban Prevention

This skill helps you understand and avoid behaviors that could get your product unfeatured, votes removed, or your account banned on Product Hunt.

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning your launch strategy
- Training team and supporters
- Reviewing outreach materials
- Checking compliance before launch
- Responding to flagged activity

## Types of Penalties

### 1. Vote Removal
- Individual votes discounted or removed
- Happens automatically via algorithm
- May not be immediately visible

### 2. Product Unfeatured
- Product removed from homepage
- Still exists but not visible
- Can happen mid-launch

### 3. Shadow Ban
- Account actions don't count
- Not notified you're banned
- Activity appears normal to you

### 4. Account Suspension
- Complete account ban
- Public or private
- May affect all associated products

## How Detection Works

### Algorithm Signals

Product Hunt's algorithm looks for:

**Account Quality:**
- Account age
- Activity history
- Engagement patterns
- Social connections

**Vote Patterns:**
- Vote timing distribution
- Geographic clustering
- Account similarity
- Velocity anomalies

**Engagement Authenticity:**
- Comment depth
- Response quality
- Discussion naturalness

### Human Review

Moderators also check for:
- Reported violations
- Unusual patterns
- Community complaints
- Obvious manipulation

## Risk Assessment Checklist

### Before Launch

**High Risk (Don't Do):**
- [ ] Are you using any vote service?
- [ ] Are you offering rewards for votes?
- [ ] Are you asking directly for upvotes?
- [ ] Are new accounts being created to vote?

**Medium Risk (Be Careful):**
- [ ] Are you sending mass emails at once?
- [ ] Are all supporters voting same time?
- [ ] Do all comments look similar?
- [ ] Are you messaging PH users directly?

**Low Risk (But Stay Alert):**
- [ ] Is your product clearly explained?
- [ ] Are your claims accurate?
- [ ] Is your offer clearly communicated?

### During Launch

**Watch For:**
- Sudden drops in vote count
- Product disappearing from homepage
- Comments being removed
- Unusual metric patterns

## If Something Goes Wrong

### If Votes Are Removed

**Don't:**
- Panic
- Spam more outreach
- Create new accounts
- Complain publicly

**Do:**
- Review your practices
- Continue quality engagement
- Focus on genuine community
- Accept the result

### If Product Is Unfeatured

**Steps:**
1. Stop all outreach immediately
2. Review potential violations
3. Contact PH support (calmly, factually)
4. Prepare explanation if asked
5. Learn for next time

### If Account Is Flagged

**Steps:**
1. Do not create new accounts
2. Contact support@producthunt.com
3. Be honest about practices
4. Accept the decision
5. Rebuild reputation over time
