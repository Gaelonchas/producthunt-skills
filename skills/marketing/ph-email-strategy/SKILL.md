# Product Hunt Email Strategy

This skill helps you plan and execute email campaigns that drive engagement for your Product Hunt launch while staying compliant with community guidelines.

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning pre-launch email sequence
- Writing launch day email campaigns
- Timing email waves across timezones
- Crafting compliant outreach messages
- Creating follow-up email sequences

## The Golden Rules

### Rule 1: Never Ask for Upvotes
- ‚ùå "Please upvote our product"
- ‚ùå "Every vote counts"
- ‚ùå "Help us reach #1"
- ‚úì "Check it out and share your feedback"
- ‚úì "We'd love your support"
- ‚úì "Let us know what you think"

### Rule 2: Stagger Your Sends
- Don't send to everyone at once
- Algorithm flags sudden traffic spikes
- Spread across 5-6 waves over 24 hours

### Rule 3: Segment Your Audience
- Different messages for different groups
- Personal > mass email
- Quality engagement > quantity

### Launch Day Emails (The 5-6 Wave Strategy)

**Wave 1: Midnight PST (for Night Owls / APAC)**
```
Recipients: Australia, Asia, Night owl friends
Time: 12:01 AM - 1:00 AM PST
```

**Wave 2: Early Morning PST (EU Afternoon)**
```
Recipients: Europe (afternoon for them)
Time: 5:00 AM - 6:00 AM PST
```

**Wave 3: Morning PST (US East Coast)**
```
Recipients: US East Coast, Canada
Time: 8:00 AM - 9:00 AM PST
```

**Wave 4: Lunch PST (US Coverage)**
```
Recipients: General US audience
Time: 12:00 PM - 1:00 PM PST
```

**Wave 5: Afternoon PST (Reminder)**
```
Recipients: Non-openers from earlier waves
Time: 4:00 PM - 5:00 PM PST
```

**Wave 6: Evening PST (Final Push)**
```
Recipients: Closest supporters, team networks
Time: 8:00 PM - 9:00 PM PST
```

## Subject Line Formulas

### High-Open-Rate Patterns
- `[Product] is live on Product Hunt`
- `I just launched! üöÄ`
- `Today's the day`
- `Quick favor? (launching today)`
- `We made something for you`

### Avoid
- ‚ùå All caps
- ‚ùå Multiple emojis
- ‚ùå "URGENT" or "IMPORTANT"

## Segmentation Strategy

### Tier 1: Inner Circle (50-100 people)
- Co-founders, close friends, family
- Early beta users
- Advisors, investors
- **Approach:** Very personal, handwritten feel

### Tier 2: Active Community (100-500 people)
- Current customers
- Newsletter subscribers
- Social media engaged
- **Approach:** Warm but efficient

### Tier 3: Extended Network (500+ people)
- General mailing list
- Past contacts
- Industry connections
- **Approach:** Professional, clear value
