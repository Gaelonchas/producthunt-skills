# Product Hunt Social Media Launch

This skill helps you coordinate social media campaigns across platforms to drive awareness and engagement for your Product Hunt launch.

## When to Use This Skill

- Planning social media content calendar
- Writing platform-specific posts
- Timing posts throughout launch day
- Creating shareable assets for supporters
- Building momentum across platforms

## Platform Strategy Overview

| Platform | Best For | Audience | Post Frequency |
|----------|----------|----------|----------------|
| Twitter/X | Real-time updates | Tech, Makers | 5-8 posts/day |
| LinkedIn | B2B, Professional | Business | 2-3 posts/day |
| Facebook | Communities, Groups | Varied | 1-2 posts/day |
| Reddit | Niche communities | Specific | 1-2 posts total |
| Indie Hackers | Makers | Founders | 1 detailed post |

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Twitter/X
- Best times: 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM (audience TZ)
- Use threads for longer stories
- Quote tweet supporter mentions
- Pin launch tweet to profile

### LinkedIn
- Best times: 8 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM (business hours)
- Long-form posts perform well
- Engagement in first hour matters
- Encourage employees to share

### Facebook
- Best for: Groups related to your niche
- Personal profile often better than page
- Don't spam groups (one thoughtful post)

### Reddit
- Find relevant subreddits in advance
- Follow subreddit rules carefully
- Provide value, not just promotion
- Be a genuine community member
